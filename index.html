Caitlin C. Nielson · Senior UX Researcher Available April 2026

Senior UX researcher who designs research programs, builds the infrastructure to scale them, and uses AI to make insights impossible to ignore.

I study how people think about money, navigate AI, and file their taxes. Then I build the tools to keep studying it, faster.

01 Who ~/ccn.md

A decade across Apple, Google, and Intuit — currently senior researcher in Intuit's Consumer Group (TurboTax, Credit Karma, Connectors).

Specialized in fintech, personal finance, and AI product behavior — usability, trust, and the decisions people make under pressure.

Cultural anthropology background; documentary work in Ecuador reached 30,000+ viewers and earned a Routledge citation. I run a team-of-one practice the same way — field-first, infrastructure-aware, built to scale without me in the loop.
